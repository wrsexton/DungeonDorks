About

  • What is this website about?

    This website is a collection of tools and utilities for assisting players of the popular Dungeons and Dragons tabletop game - owned by wizards of the coast.

  • What is Dungeons and Dragons?

    Dungeons and Dragons is a tabletop RPG - a type of game played using a set of core rulebooks and the imaginations of a group of friends coming together to have an adventure!

  • How do I use this website?

    Use the navigation buttons at the top of the site to find tools and other content.

  • Where can I find more information?

    You can find more information about Wizards of the Coast and their various games at their website.
